\begin{abstract}

This paper aims to present, using study of case, the compilance levels for
information security that can be applied into a company using the Brazilian
standard NBR ISO/IEC 17799:2001 {--} part 1. His achievement involved
literature search and field, which had focused
the security controls of a division of the pharmaceutical industry and has
as challenge to maintain the security of your information.

The field research is based in the Brazilian standard NBR ISO/IEC 17799:2001.
Were also considered especialized authors in information security area for
interpretation of results.

Is displayed in this work a study of case that shows the eficiency of the
security controller into a pharmaceutic industry. The field research was
developed to avaliate the compilance level of this company on the standard.
Are also displayed studies about information security, contract general theory
and information security into contract management.

\vspace{5cm}
\end{abstract}
